Pizza

Pizza, especially the classic Margherita, originates from Naples, Italy, where it started as a simple flatbread topped with tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. The combination of chewy crust, rich tomato sauce, and creamy cheese makes for a balanced and universally loved flavor profile. The Margherita, with its red, white, and green colors, is said to have been created in 1889 in honor of Queen Margherita of Savoy.
